# ------------------------------------------------------------
# VR/AR Game Design Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: vr-ar-checklist
  name: VR/AR Game Design Checklist
  description: Ensure game design provides comfortable and immersive VR/AR experiences.
items:
  - "[ ] Motion sickness prevention measures implemented"
  - "[ ] Comfortable movement and locomotion systems designed"
  - "[ ] Hand tracking and gesture controls are intuitive"
  - "[ ] Spatial audio enhances immersion appropriately"
  - "[ ] UI elements are positioned comfortably in 3D space"
  - "[ ] Safety boundaries and play area warnings implemented"
  - "[ ] Performance maintains required frame rates for comfort"
  - "[ ] Accessibility options for different physical abilities"
  - "[ ] Calibration and setup processes are user-friendly"
  - "[ ] Cross-platform compatibility addressed (if applicable)"
  - "[ ] Real-world integration works seamlessly (AR)"
  - "[ ] Environmental occlusion and lighting realistic (AR)"
  - "[ ] Social features support shared experiences"
  - "[ ] Content warnings for intense experiences provided"
  - "[ ] Platform-specific features utilized effectively"
