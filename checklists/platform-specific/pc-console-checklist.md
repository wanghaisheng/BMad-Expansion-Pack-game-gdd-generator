# ------------------------------------------------------------
# PC & Console Game Design Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: pc-console-checklist
  name: PC & Console Game Design Checklist
  description: Ensure game design leverages PC and console platform strengths and meets platform requirements.
items:
  - "[ ] Keyboard and mouse controls are precise and customizable"
  - "[ ] Controller support is fully implemented and tested"
  - "[ ] UI scales appropriately for large screens and TV viewing distances"
  - "[ ] Graphics settings provide scalability across hardware configurations"
  - "[ ] Performance targets utilize platform capabilities effectively"
  - "[ ] Platform-specific features are integrated (achievements, cloud saves)"
  - "[ ] Modding support considered and implemented (if applicable)"
  - "[ ] Multiple display and aspect ratio support verified"
  - "[ ] Platform certification requirements met"
  - "[ ] DLC and expansion content strategy planned"
  - "[ ] Streaming and content creation features supported"
  - "[ ] Advanced audio features utilized (surround sound, spatial audio)"
  - "[ ] High-fidelity graphics and effects implemented"
  - "[ ] Long-form gameplay sessions supported comfortably"
  - "[ ] Platform-specific social features integrated"
...
