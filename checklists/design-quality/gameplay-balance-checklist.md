# ------------------------------------------------------------
# Gameplay Balance Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: gameplay-balance-checklist
  name: Gameplay Balance Checklist
  description: Ensure game mechanics are balanced and provide fair, engaging challenges.
items:
  - "[ ] Player abilities scale appropriately with enemy difficulty"
  - "[ ] Resource economy prevents exploitation or starvation"
  - "[ ] Economic health metrics monitored（inflation, faucets/sinks balance）"
  - "[ ] Risk vs reward ratios are meaningful and fair"
  - "[ ] No single strategy dominates all situations"
  - "[ ] Difficulty curve provides steady progression"
  - "[ ] Player choices have meaningful consequences"
  - "[ ] Failure states provide learning opportunities"
  - "[ ] Success feels earned rather than given"
  - "[ ] Randomness enhances rather than undermines skill"
  - "[ ] Accessibility options don't break core balance"
  - "[ ] VIP & Loyalty systems remain fair and non-disruptive"
...
