# 2. Game World Design Continuity Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: game-world-design-continuity-checklist
  name: Game World Design Continuity Checklist
  description: Ensure level geography, game systems, mechanics rules, and progression timeline stay coherent.
items:
  - "[ ] Level geography and layout referenced consistently"
  - "[ ] Game system rules and mechanics remain uniform"
  - "[ ] Gameplay limitations and constraints not violated"
  - "[ ] Progression milestones and timeline match design documents"
  - "[ ] Resource economy and balance align across all systems"
  - "[ ] Travel/movement mechanics and distances are plausible"
  - "[ ] Environmental storytelling elements are consistent"
  - "[ ] Technical performance requirements align with world complexity"
