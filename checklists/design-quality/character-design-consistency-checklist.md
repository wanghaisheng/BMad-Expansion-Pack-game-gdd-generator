# ------------------------------------------------------------
# 1. Character Design Consistency Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: character-design-consistency-checklist
  name: Character Design Consistency Checklist
  description: Verify character designs and gameplay functions remain consistent throughout the game design document.
items:
  - "[ ] Character names and aliases spelled consistently"
  - "[ ] Visual descriptions match across all design documents"
  - "[ ] Gameplay abilities and stats do not contradict earlier specifications"
  - "[ ] Character personality and dialogue style is uniform"
  - "[ ] Relationships and backstories align with game timeline"
  - "[ ] Character progression/arc is logical and achievable"
  - "[ ] Animation and interaction requirements are consistent"
  - "[ ] Audio design (voice, sounds) specifications match"
...
