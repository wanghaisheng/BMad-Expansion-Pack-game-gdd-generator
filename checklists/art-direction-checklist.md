# ------------------------------------------------------------
# Art Direction Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: art-direction-checklist
  name: Art Direction Checklist
  description: Ensure visual art direction is consistent, appealing, and supports gameplay objectives.
items:
  - "[ ] Art style is consistent across all game elements"
  - "[ ] Visual theme supports and enhances gameplay mechanics"
  - "[ ] Color palette is cohesive and purposeful"
  - "[ ] Character designs are distinctive and memorable"
  - "[ ] Environment art supports navigation and gameplay"
  - "[ ] UI art integrates seamlessly with game world aesthetics"
  - "[ ] Technical art requirements are achievable within budget"
  - "[ ] Art assets are optimized for target platforms"
  - "[ ] Visual effects enhance gameplay without overwhelming"
  - "[ ] Lighting design supports mood and gameplay clarity"
  - "[ ] Art direction accommodates localization needs"
  - "[ ] Accessibility considerations are integrated into visual design"
  - "[ ] Art pipeline supports efficient production workflow"
  - "[ ] Asset pipeline and naming rules defined (prefixes, folders, variants)"
  - "[ ] Asset list created with required fields and coverage by category"
  - "[ ] Asset metadata schema and validation gates defined (CI-ready)"
  - "[ ] Media profiles defined for video/image_sequence/sprite_sheet/audio"
  - "[ ] Visual storytelling complements narrative elements"
  - "[ ] Art style appeals to target audience demographics"
...
