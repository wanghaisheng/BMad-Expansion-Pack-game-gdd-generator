# ------------------------------------------------------------
# Market Readiness Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: market-readiness-checklist
  name: Market Readiness Checklist
  description: Ensure game design is positioned for market success and competitive advantage.
items:
  - "[ ] Target audience is clearly defined and validated"
  - "[ ] Competitive analysis identifies clear differentiation"
  - "[ ] Market size and opportunity are quantified"
  - "[ ] Pricing strategy is competitive and justified"
  - "[ ] Marketing hooks and unique selling points are compelling"
  - "[ ] Platform strategy maximizes market reach"
  - "[ ] Localization plan covers key markets"
  - "[ ] Launch timing considers market conditions and competition"
  - "[ ] User acquisition strategy is cost-effective"
  - "[ ] Retention and monetization projections are realistic"
  - "[ ] Community ecosystem strategy supports long-term growth"
  - "[ ] Brand positioning is clear and consistent"
  - "[ ] Community building strategy supports long-term growth"
  - "[ ] Influencer and content creator appeal is considered"
  - "[ ] Regulatory compliance is verified for all target markets"
  - "[ ] Success metrics and KPIs are defined and trackable"
...
