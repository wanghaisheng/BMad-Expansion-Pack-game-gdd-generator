# ------------------------------------------------------------
# Casual & Puzzle Game Design Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: casual-puzzle-checklist
  name: Casual & Puzzle Game Design Checklist
  description: Ensure casual and puzzle games are accessible, engaging, and provide appropriate challenge progression.
items:
  - "[ ] Core mechanics are immediately understandable"
  - "[ ] Tutorial introduces concepts gradually and clearly"
  - "[ ] Difficulty progression is smooth and well-paced"
  - "[ ] Hint systems help without trivializing puzzles"
  - "[ ] Visual design is clear and uncluttered"
  - "[ ] Audio feedback enhances the experience"
  - "[ ] Sessions can be short and satisfying"
  - "[ ] Progress is saved and resumable"
  - "[ ] Accessibility options accommodate diverse players"
  - "[ ] Social features encourage sharing and competition"
  - "[ ] Monetization is fair and non-intrusive"
  - "[ ] Content variety prevents repetition"
  - "[ ] Failure states are encouraging rather than punishing"
  - "[ ] Achievement systems provide additional goals"
  - "[ ] Replayability is built into core design"
...
