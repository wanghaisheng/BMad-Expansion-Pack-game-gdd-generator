# ------------------------------------------------------------
# Multiplayer Game Design Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: multiplayer-game-checklist
  name: Multiplayer Game Design Checklist
  description: Ensure multiplayer games provide fair, engaging, and socially positive experiences.
items:
  - "[ ] Matchmaking creates balanced and fair matches"
  - "[ ] Network latency compensation works effectively"
  - "[ ] Anti-cheat systems are implemented and effective"
  - "[ ] Social features encourage positive interactions"
  - "[ ] Reporting and moderation systems handle toxic behavior"
  - "[ ] Communication tools are appropriate for the game type"
  - "[ ] Progression systems work fairly in multiplayer context"
  - "[ ] Server infrastructure can handle expected player loads"
  - "[ ] Disconnection handling preserves game integrity"
  - "[ ] Cross-platform play works seamlessly (if applicable)"
  - "[ ] Spectator modes enhance community engagement"
  - "[ ] Ranking systems accurately reflect player skill"
  - "[ ] Team formation and management tools are effective"
  - "[ ] Privacy and safety features protect players"
  - "[ ] Community features foster long-term engagement"
...
