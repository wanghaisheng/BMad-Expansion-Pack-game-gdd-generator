# ------------------------------------------------------------
# Comprehensive Game Design Quality Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: comprehensive-quality-checklist
  name: Comprehensive Game Design Quality Checklist
  description: Master checklist covering all aspects of game design quality and readiness.
items:
  # Core Design Quality
  - "[ ] Game concept is clear, compelling, and well-defined"
  - "[ ] Core gameplay loop is engaging and sustainable"
  - "[ ] Player progression provides meaningful advancement"
  - "[ ] Game mechanics are balanced and fair"
  - "[ ] Difficulty curve is appropriate and well-paced"
  
  # Technical Excellence
  - "[ ] Performance targets are realistic and achievable"
  - "[ ] Technical architecture supports all planned features"
  - "[ ] Platform requirements are clearly specified"
  - "[ ] Scalability and optimization strategies are defined"
  - "[ ] BMAD compatibility requirements are met"
  - "[ ] AI behavior visuals provided（State Machines/Behavior Trees）"
  
  # User Experience
  - "[ ] UI/UX design is intuitive and accessible"
  - "[ ] Onboarding effectively teaches core mechanics"
  - "[ ] Onboarding & Paywall flows are clearly designed"
  - "[ ] Player feedback systems are comprehensive"
  - "[ ] Accessibility features accommodate diverse players"
  - "[ ] Localization requirements are thoroughly planned"
  - "[ ] UX philosophy is defined（Curiosity/Self-Narrative）"
  
  # Business Viability
  - "[ ] Target market is clearly defined and validated"
  - "[ ] Monetization strategy is ethical and sustainable"
  - "[ ] Economic architecture is defined with health metrics"
  - "[ ] VIP & Loyalty system design is fair and transparent"
  - "[ ] Competitive positioning is strong and differentiated"
  - "[ ] Development scope is realistic for available resources"
  - "[ ] Post-launch support and content strategy is planned"
  
  # Cultural and Social
  - "[ ] Content is culturally sensitive and appropriate"
  - "[ ] Diversity and inclusion are thoughtfully represented"
  - "[ ] Community features promote positive interactions"
  - "[ ] Community ecosystem is designed（Internal + External platforms）"
  - "[ ] Social impact and responsibility are considered"
  - "[ ] Regional compliance and regulations are addressed"
  
  # Long-term Success
  - "[ ] Live operations strategy supports ongoing engagement"
  - "[ ] Retention strategy defined（3B model + 7-day behavior table）"
  - "[ ] Community building and management plans are established"
  - "[ ] Data collection and analysis frameworks are implemented"
  - "[ ] Iteration and improvement processes are defined"
  - "[ ] Success metrics and KPIs are clearly established"
...
