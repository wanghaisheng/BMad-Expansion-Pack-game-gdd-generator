checklist:
  id: cultural-sensitivity-checklist
  name: Cultural Sensitivity Checklist
  description: Ensure game content is culturally appropriate and respectful for target audiences.
items:
  - "[ ] Cultural symbols and references used accurately and respectfully"
  - "[ ] No cultural stereotypes or offensive representations"
  - "[ ] Religious and spiritual content handled sensitively"
  - "[ ] Historical events portrayed with appropriate context"
  - "[ ] Character designs avoid cultural appropriation"
  - "[ ] Language and dialogue culturally appropriate"
  - "[ ] Color symbolism considered for target cultures"
  - "[ ] Gesture and body language culturally appropriate"
  - "[ ] Food, clothing, and customs accurately represented"
  - "[ ] Sensitive topics handled with care or avoided"
  - "[ ] Local cultural consultants involved in review"
  - "[ ] Regional content rating requirements met"
