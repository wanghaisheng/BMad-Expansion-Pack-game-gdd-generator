# ------------------------------------------------------------
# Localization Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: localization-checklist
  name: Localization Checklist
  description: Ensure game design supports effective localization and cultural adaptation.
items:
  - "[ ] Text expansion and contraction are accommodated in UI design"
  - "[ ] Cultural symbols and references are appropriate for target regions"
  - "[ ] Color meanings and associations are considered for different cultures"
  - "[ ] Religious and political sensitivities are addressed"
  - "[ ] Local regulations and content ratings are complied with"
  - "[ ] Currency and payment methods are localized"
  - "[ ] Date, time, and number formats follow local conventions"
  - "[ ] Character encoding supports all target languages"
  - "[ ] Font selection supports all required character sets"
  - "[ ] Audio localization requirements are planned and budgeted"
  - "[ ] Cultural adaptation goes beyond translation"
  - "[ ] Local market preferences are reflected in gameplay"
  - "[ ] Testing with native speakers is planned"
  - "[ ] Localization workflow and tools are established"
  - "[ ] Update and maintenance processes include localization"
...
