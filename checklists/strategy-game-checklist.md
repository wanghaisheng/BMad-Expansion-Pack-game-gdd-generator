# ------------------------------------------------------------
# Strategy Game Design Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: strategy-game-checklist
  name: Strategy Game Design Checklist
  description: Ensure strategy game design provides deep, balanced, and engaging strategic gameplay.
items:
  - "[ ] Multiple viable strategies and win conditions exist"
  - "[ ] Resource management creates meaningful decisions"
  - "[ ] Unit balance prevents dominant strategies"
  - "[ ] Information presentation is clear and comprehensive"
  - "[ ] AI opponents provide appropriate challenge levels"
  - "[ ] Turn structure (if turn-based) flows smoothly"
  - "[ ] Real-time elements (if RTS) are manageable"
  - "[ ] Map design supports strategic variety"
  - "[ ] Technology/research trees offer meaningful choices"
  - "[ ] Economic systems are balanced and engaging"
  - "[ ] Diplomatic options (if applicable) are meaningful"
  - "[ ] Learning curve is manageable for new players"
  - "[ ] Advanced strategies reward skilled players"
  - "[ ] Multiplayer balance is thoroughly tested"
  - "[ ] Replay value is high through strategic variety"
...
