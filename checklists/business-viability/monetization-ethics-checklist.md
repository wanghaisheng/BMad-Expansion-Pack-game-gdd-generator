# ------------------------------------------------------------
# Monetization Ethics Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: monetization-ethics-checklist
  name: Monetization Ethics Checklist
  description: Ensure monetization systems are ethical, fair, and player-friendly.
items:
  - "[ ] Monetization does not create pay-to-win scenarios"
  - "[ ] Free players have meaningful progression paths"
  - "[ ] Paid content provides clear value without exploitation"
  - "[ ] No predatory monetization targeting vulnerable players"
  - "[ ] Transparent pricing and purchase information"
  - "[ ] Reasonable spending limits and warnings"
  - "[ ] No gambling mechanics without proper disclosure"
  - "[ ] Child protection measures implemented"
  - "[ ] Refund policies clearly stated"
  - "[ ] Monetization complies with regional regulations"
  - "[ ] Player data and privacy protected in payment systems"
  - "[ ] VIP tiers avoid power gaps that damage competitive integrity"
  - "[ ] Loyalty rewards prioritize recognition over coercion"
...
