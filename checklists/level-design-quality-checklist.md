# ------------------------------------------------------------
# 3. Level Design Quality Checklist
# ------------------------------------------------------------
---
checklist:
  id: level-design-quality-checklist
  name: Level Design Quality Checklist
  description: Quick QA pass for each level/area to ensure gameplay purpose and flow.
items:
  - "[ ] Clear player objective established immediately"
  - "[ ] Level challenge & core mechanic articulated"
  - "[ ] Stakes and consequences apparent to player"
  - "[ ] Engaging opening and satisfying conclusion"
  - "[ ] Logical progression from previous level"
  - "[ ] Player feedback and reward systems present"
  - "[ ] Difficulty curve appropriate for placement in game"
  - "[ ] Environmental storytelling supports game world"
...
